AI Search · Multi-Client
AEO/GEO — Optimizing for ChatGPT, Gemini & AI Overviews
AEO GEO AI SEO
›
Context

As AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Gemini, Google AI Overviews) began reshaping how users discover businesses, I identified this shift early and built a systematic methodology for optimizing client websites to appear in AI-generated answers — a practice now known as Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

Methodology
  • Rewrote website copy to match conversational, natural language query patterns used in AI prompts
  • Structured content using FAQ schemas, clear heading hierarchies, and concise answer formats
  • Implemented structured data (JSON-LD) to improve AI crawler comprehension
  • Optimized for featured snippets and People Also Ask as precursors to AI citation
  • Built entity authority by ensuring consistent NAP data and authoritative mentions across the web
  • Monitored brand mentions in AI responses using manual querying and emerging monitoring tools
Why This Matters

The shift from keyword-based to AI-mediated search is the biggest change in organic marketing since Google's Hummingbird update. Brands that appear in ChatGPT and Gemini answers gain visibility in a zero-click environment where traditional SEO rankings are irrelevant.

Outcome

Applied across multiple client websites, resulting in measurable boosts in organic traffic. One of only a small number of SEO specialists in India with documented, hands-on AEO/GEO implementation experience at scale.
